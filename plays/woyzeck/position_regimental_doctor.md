# Position: The Regimental Doctor

## Taxonomy

A seat among the garrison's medical staff: licensed by the service to keep men fit
and, in the keeping, to study them.

## Owner

- Project: Woyzeck

## Has

It brings the authority of science, the keys to the few groschen a poor soldier
will chase, and a living body it may treat as a case: whoever holds it commands
the means to make a man agree to be an experiment.

## Orders

It orders him to keep the ranks serviceable, to record and measure, and to run
[the pea diet](process_the_pea_diet.md) to its conclusion whatever it does to the
man underneath it.

## Loses

It strips him of ordinary compassion and of the power to see [Franz Woyzeck](persona_woyzeck.md)
as a man rather than a result: the seat rewards the reading that turns suffering
into data, so the plain human sight is what it costs.

## Drives

Holding it, he drives the experiment forward, the steady optimisation of a body
toward the proof he wants, until the instrument that carries the theory is used up
by carrying it.
