# Position: The Drum Major

## Taxonomy

The head of the regiment's band and the front of its parade: a seat made of being
seen, standing apart from the line ranks it outshines.

## Owner

- Project: Woyzeck

## Has

It brings the plumes, the front of the column, the eyes of the whole town, and a
physical command that needs no argument: whoever holds it is admired before he has
done anything.

## Orders

It orders him to lead the band, to be looked at, and to carry the swagger the
regiment wants to see in itself, taking the admiration as his right.

## Loses

It strips him of any need for an inner life: the seat is all surface and rewards
the surface, so it costs him the reason to be anything underneath the figure the
town applauds.

## Drives

Holding it, he drives the spectacle and the appetite that comes with it: the
parade, the tavern, and the certainty that what he wants, including [Marie](persona_marie.md),
is already owed to him.
