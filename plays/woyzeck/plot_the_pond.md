# Plot: The Pond

## Taxonomy

One movement of the production [Woyzeck](play_woyzeck.md): the close, where the
last thing he owns is spent and there is nothing after it.

## Owner

- Project: Woyzeck

## Cue

[the pond](place_the_pond.md) at the edge of the town after dark: black standing
water, low trees, mist off the marsh, a red moon low enough to touch. The cold
has emptied the place of everyone but the two of them. This is the air the ending
begins in, settled and waiting before either of them speaks.

## Action

[the killing](process_the_killing.md) runs to its end. [Franz Woyzeck](persona_woyzeck.md),
still wearing the seat of [the subaltern soldier](position_subaltern_soldier.md)
that taught him to do what he is told, leads [Marie](persona_marie.md) out along
the water on the pretext of a walk. There is no collision of equals here, only
one vector closing: a man who has been spent down to nothing spending the last
thing left to him, with the woman who was that thing walking beside him.

## Stage

The two stand at the reeds where the path gives out. [Marie](persona_marie.md)
has stopped, sensing the walk has gone too far. [Franz Woyzeck](persona_woyzeck.md)
has his hand in his coat on [the knife](piece_the_knife.md). The arrangement is
the murder in miniature: deep water on one side, the way back on the other, and
his body set between her and it.

## Tension

[Franz Woyzeck](persona_woyzeck.md) carries the thing that cannot be argued out
of him: the voices that have told him for nights to do it, and [the knife](piece_the_knife.md)
he bought cheap for the one use. He is no longer deciding; he is obeying, the way
he was trained to, only the order now comes from inside the ruin the peas and the
betrayal made. The knife holds the whole unpaid weight of the run, and when it
moves the stakes are gone.
