# Persona: Franz Woyzeck

## Taxonomy

The role he fills is [the subaltern soldier](position_subaltern_soldier.md): the
bottom seat of the garrison, the one the others are licensed to use.

## Owner

- Project: Woyzeck

## Projection

A thin, hollow-cheeked man in a uniform worn shiny at the cuffs, holding
[the subaltern soldier](position_subaltern_soldier.md) and carrying
[the razor](piece_the_razor.md) that is his trade. He shows the room a willing
servant: quick on his feet, eager to oblige, agreeing before the order is
finished, a poor man trying hard to be no trouble to anyone.

## Action

He runs [the shaving](process_the_shaving.md) over the Captain with a steady
practised hand, crosses the town on errands for a few groschen, lays his wages on
[Marie](persona_marie.md)'s table, and presses his ear to the ground in the field
to listen to what is speaking under the grass.

## Shadow

Under the obliging face is a man on fire: he sees the crust of the world gone thin
and a furnace beneath it, hears voices come up out of the earth telling him the
thing must be done, and is at most half aware that the visions are not the world
reporting itself but his own mind coming apart. The willing servant and the man
receiving orders from the ground cannot both be true, and he cannot see the seam.

## Tell

A hard twitch starts at the corner of his right eye and his hands shake whenever
he is made to stand still and be looked at. His breath catches in the middle of a
sentence and he loses the thread, an involuntary leak of the panic the obedient
face is built to hide.
