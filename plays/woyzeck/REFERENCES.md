# Woyzeck Language: Reference

## Line of Work

Language as **exhaust and diagnostic**: the phonetic and structural materialization of pressure. The domain models Georg Buechner's radical break from classical drama: language is not a tool the persona commands, but a symptom the environment forces out of them. The subaltern's language cracks under the load, while institutional power speaks in closed, frictionless loops.

## Origin

Buechner's *Woyzeck* fragments (1836/1837) and the historical shift toward social realism in dramatic speech.

| Source | Key Work | Scope |
| - | - | - |
| **Georg Buechner** | *Woyzeck* drafts | The raw, paratactic, dialect-inflected speech of the subaltern. Repetition as a sign of mental overload. |
| **Clinical / Military Records** | The Clarus Report | The cold, objectifying vocabulary of institutional power (Latinate medical terms, moralizing maxims). |

## Restrictions

What the language reference refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Grammatical correction**: The production refuses to clean or standardise Woyzeck's syntax. The missing verbs and fractured clauses are load-bearing architecture, not errors.
- **Universal eloquence**: The play does not grant Woyzeck the vocabulary to analyze his own condition. He can only describe the symptoms ("Ein Feuer faehrt um den Himmel"). The analysis is delegated to the architecture itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per file.

- **[Franz Woyzeck](persona_woyzeck.md)**: Encoded to speak in fragments, repetitions ("immer zu"), and stark physical imagery. He operates at the edge of his *Using Language* widths, often failing to bridge the gap between thinking and speaking.
- **[The Doctor](persona_the_doctor.md)**: Encoded to use Latinate clinical terms and possessive pronouns ("mein Fall", "mein Woyzeck"). His language does not converse; it observes and measures.
- **[The Captain](persona_the_captain.md)**: Encoded to speak slowly, heavily, and in moralistic circles ("Ein guter Mensch"). His language fills the empty time he fears.
- **[Marie](persona_marie.md)**: Encoded to swing between earthly, sensual expressions and elevated Biblical guilt. Her language breaks when the two collide.
