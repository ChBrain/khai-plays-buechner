# Persona: Marie

## Taxonomy

One of the women the garrison keeps: a soldier's common-law wife and the mother
of his child, a seat the muster roll does not record and the town does not
respect. The role is real but has no rank, so it is named here rather than held
as a position.

## Owner

- Project: Woyzeck

## Projection

A striking young woman with dark hair, making a bare room look like a home,
turning [the earrings](piece_the_earrings.md) to catch what light there is. She
shows the world a good mother and a loyal woman: the child fed, her hair done,
her man's supper kept, getting through poverty on will and vitality.

## Action

She rocks the child's cradle and sings to it, leans at the window to watch the
parade go by, combs her hair at the cracked glass, and turns the pages of the
Bible to the woman taken in adultery, reading the mercy at the end of it aloud to
herself.

## Shadow

Under the good mother is a hunger she will not look at straight: for warmth, for a
little brightness, for a man who is admired and whole, and a dread of staying poor
forever that pulls hard against her loyalty. She tells herself she is faithful
while she is already gone, and cannot hold both, so she holds neither cleanly.

## Tell

Her hand goes to cover the earrings when Woyzeck's eyes find them, and she drops
her gaze to the floorboards whenever a shadow fills the doorway. She defends
herself before anyone has said a word, which says the word for her.
