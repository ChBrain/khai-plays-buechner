# Management Order: Staging of Leonce und Lena

## Direction

Georg Büchner's comedy _Leonce und Lena_ represents the absolute peak of existential satire in the 19th century. In a world of petty absolute kingdoms, the characters struggle against boredom and the inevitability of their pre-destined roles. Staging this work is our civic duty to demonstrate the parodic automation of the aristocracy.

## Orders

1. **The Playwright (buechner)** shall model the ENACTS structure for _Leonce und Lena_ in German, capturing the precise literary voice of the characters, places, and processes.
2. **The Theatre Manager (franzos)** shall ensure the directory structure passes all guard checks, formatting constraints, and that all files use LF line endings.
3. **The Theatre Manager (franzos)** shall verify that the reference file conforms to the LORE guidelines and that all links resolve correctly.

## Implementation

- **Location:** The files must reside in the `plays/leonce_und_lena/` directory.
- **Language:**
  - Administrative and structural documents (`order_260606.md`, `REFERENCES.md`) are in English.
  - Play content (personas, positions, pieces, places, processes, plots, and main play file) is in German.
- **Templates:** Use the standard ENACTS markdown structures.
